I just read the 'Attention Is All You Need' paper. It's the one that introduced the Transformer architecture. The key insight is using self-attention mechanisms instead of recurrence or convolutions for sequence transduction. This feels like the foundational idea behind a lot of the models I use today, like those in the [[tag-transformers]] space. The paper itself is [[paper-attention-is-all-you-need]].
