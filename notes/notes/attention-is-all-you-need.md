I read the paper "Attention Is All You Need" by Vaswani et al. (2017). It proposed the [[paper-attention-is-all-you-need]] Transformer architecture, which replaced RNNs and LSTMs with self-attention. The key insight was that attention is sufficient for sequence transduction. This fundamentally changed NLP and led to models like BERT and GPT. The parallelization advantage over recurrent models is huge. It's the foundation for most modern LLMs, including approaches like [[test-time-scaling]].
